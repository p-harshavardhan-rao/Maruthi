Our Story

From Spices to Steel & Concrete

What began as a spices trading enterprise grew through logistics into one of India’s most respected infrastructure material manufacturers. Every pole we produce carries the legacy of decades of entrepreneurial grit.

Maruthi PSCC Poles was established by Pusukuri Rama Rao in Sirisilla, Telangana. The company specialises in Pre-Stressed Cement Concrete (PSCC) Poles for electricity distribution networks across India — powering rural and urban areas alike through government and private infrastructure projects.

I

Business Origins — Spices Trade

The founding family begins commercial operations in agricultural commodities, building capital and business acumen across Telangana.

II

Expansion into Logistics

Recognising the power of controlled supply chains, the company builds its own trucking fleet — solving last-mile delivery challenges before most manufacturers do.

III

2021 — Maruthi PSCC Poles Founded

The company pivots into PSCC Pole manufacturing, combining logistics expertise with high-capacity production in Sirisilla.

IV

Today — National Scale

2.75+ million poles produced annually. Supplying government power departments and infrastructure companies across India with an owned fleet delivering nationwide.

Why Our Logistics Make the Difference

PSCC Poles are heavy, fragile in transit, and difficult to handle. Most manufacturers outsource delivery — creating delays, breakage, and accountability gaps. Maruthi PSCC Poles owns its logistics.

With approximately 80 trucks in our own fleet, we manage pick-up from our Sirisilla plant, transit across Indian state highways, and delivery directly to project sites — anywhere in India.

This integrated model means fewer delays, lower costs, and zero third-party blame.
