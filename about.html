// The Journey

Building India's Power Infrastructure

Maruthi PSCC Poles was established by Pusukuri Rama Rao in Sirisilla, Telangana in 2021. What began as a spices trading enterprise grew through logistics into one of India's most respected infrastructure material manufacturers.

The company specialises in Pre-Stressed Cement Concrete (PSCC) Poles for electricity distribution networks across India — powering rural and urban areas alike through government and private infrastructure projects.

Every pole we produce carries the legacy of decades of entrepreneurial determination — and the mission to bring electricity to every corner of India.

// Company History

Our Journey

Four pivotal chapters that define who we are today.

Chapter I
Business Origins — Spices Trade
The founding family begins commercial operations in agricultural commodities, building capital and business acumen across Telangana. A foundation of enterprise and hustle.
🌾
🚚
Chapter II
Expansion into Logistics
Recognising the power of controlled supply chains, the company builds its own trucking fleet — solving last-mile delivery challenges before most manufacturers think to do so.
Chapter III · 2021
Maruthi PSCC Poles Founded
The company pivots into PSCC Pole manufacturing, combining logistics expertise with high-capacity production in Sirisilla. A transformative leap into infrastructure manufacturing.
🏭
⚡
Chapter IV · Today
National Scale
2.75+ million poles produced annually. Supplying government power departments and infrastructure companies across India with an owned fleet delivering nationwide.
