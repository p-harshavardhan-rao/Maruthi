⭐ Core Product

PSCC Poles

Pre-Stressed Cement Concrete Poles — the backbone of India's rural and urban electricity distribution networks.

  • Used in High-Tension (HT) and Low-Tension (LT) electricity lines
  • Supplied to State Power Distribution Companies (DISCOMs) across India
  • Manufactured at 2.75+ million units annually from Sirisilla plant
  • Designed to withstand extreme weather and heavy load conditions
  • Available in 7m, 8m, 9m, 10m, 11m and 12m heights
  • Manufactured as per BIS standards (IS: 1678 & IS: 2905)
  • Superior crack resistance and long service life (50+ years)

Spun Poles

High-strength centrifugally spun concrete poles for transmission and distribution infrastructure.

  • Superior compressive strength via centrifugal spinning process
  • Consistent diameter and smooth finish along full length
  • Ideal for urban power networks and transmission lines
  • Reduced maintenance requirements due to dense concrete matrix
  • Higher load capacity compared to conventional poles
  • Available in various lengths as per project specifications
  • Excellent resistance to corrosion and environmental degradation

Foundation

Spun Piles

Pre-stressed spun concrete piles for deep foundation applications in infrastructure and construction.

  • Engineered to transfer loads to deeper, more stable soil layers
  • Pre-stressing significantly increases load-bearing capacity
  • Uniform cross-section and consistent quality throughout
  • Used in bridges, flyovers, buildings, and port structures
  • Available in various diameter and length combinations
  • Faster installation compared to cast-in-situ alternatives
  • Proven performance in soft soil and waterlogged conditions
